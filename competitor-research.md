### NordVPN Key Facts (top10vpn)
- Ranked #14 for Privacy, #2 for Streaming, #5 for Speed
- Logs: date of last connection + account info only
- RAM-only servers (diskless)
- Panama jurisdiction (Nord Security HQ in Netherlands — doesn't affect jurisdiction)
- Proven in 2024 legal case — no useful data to hand over
- NordLynx = WireGuard + double NAT (marketing rebrand)
- ChaCha20 encryption + 2048-bit DH key
- Unblocks 17 Netflix libraries (auto-connects to US Netflix)
- Fails on Netflix India/Netherlands
- Best for BBC iPlayer — consistently reliable
- Speed loss: ~4% on nearby servers
- Streaming score: 9.8/10, Speed score: 9.7/10 (top10vpn methodology)

### ExpressVPN Key Facts (top10vpn)
- Ranked #7 for Privacy, #1 for Streaming, #5 for Speed
- TrustedServer = RAM-only servers
- BVI jurisdiction (Kape Technologies owner)
- Turkish server seizure proved no logs
- Logs: bandwidth usage + account info (anonymous, can't identify users)
- KPMG audit of RAM-only servers (2025)
- Unblocks 95% of 120 streaming services tested
- Unblocks 18 Netflix libraries (most of any VPN)
- Lightway protocol: 2% speed loss nearby
- Lightway Turbo available on Windows
- Speed declined 27% in South Africa, 7% in Germany recently
- 13,360 servers in 109 countries

### Surfshark Key Facts (top10vpn)
- Ranked #16 for Privacy, #3 for Streaming, #23 for Speed
- Owned by Cyberspace (merger of Surfshark + Nord Security)
- Logs connection timestamps — deleted within 15 minutes
- RAM-only servers, Deloitte audits
- Netherlands jurisdiction
- Unblocks 9 Netflix libraries
- Best choice for Disney+ UK
- GPS spoofer on Android (only VPN with this besides Windscribe)
- 4% speed loss nearby, 16% to Japan, 33% to South Africa
- 10Gbps servers being rolled out
- AES-256 (OpenVPN) or ChaCha20 (WireGuard)

## Content Structure Insights

### What makes top10vpn.com content strong:
1. Specific sub-rankings for each category (e.g. "Ranked #2 for Streaming out of 61")
2. Detailed logging tables showing exactly what IS and ISN'T logged
3. Real-world proof of privacy claims (court cases, server seizures)
4. Specific streaming service compatibility tables
5. Honest about weaknesses (e.g. NordVPN fails on Netflix India)
6. Speed loss percentages (not just raw Mbps)
7. Wireshark verification screenshots described
8. Comparison to competitors within each review
9. 30,000+ hours of testing claimed
10. 61 VPNs tested total

### Key differentiators to use in our content:
- We test from UK servers specifically (UK-focused angle)
- We note UK-specific streaming services (BBC iPlayer, ITVX, Sky Go, Channel 4)
- UK legal context (Investigatory Powers Act)
- UK pricing in GBP
- UK-based customer support where available
